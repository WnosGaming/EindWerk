Assassin's Creed Series

Welcome to the World of Assassins!

The Assassin's Creed series is an action-adventure franchise that takes players on a journey through history, putting them in the role of an assassin who must fight against the Templars to preserve freedom and liberty.

The series features an expansive open-world, rich with history and intrigue, where players can explore and discover secrets hidden throughout each game.
